There are different types of programming languages, and these different types can be divided according to the criterion of their proximity to the human language or the language of the machine, in terms of the vocabulary used in it and the basic rules used in its formulation. Programming languages are divided according to this standard into two types as follows:
  • Languages closer to machine language
  • Languages closer to the human language
On the other hand , programming languages can be divided into different types according to other criteria, such as dividing them according to the purposes or goals for which they were designed, Or divide them according to the privacy of the devices you work on and so on. The following are the details of these divisions:
